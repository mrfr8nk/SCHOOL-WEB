About Our School

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Founded in 1952, St. Mary's High School has been a cornerstone of educational excellence in our community for over 70 years. Our mission is to nurture the intellectual, spiritual, and personal growth of each student.

With a student-teacher ratio of 12:1, we provide personalized attention to ensure every student reaches their full potential. Our graduates consistently gain admission to top universities and excel in their chosen fields.

At St. Mary's, we believe education extends beyond the classroom. Our comprehensive programs in arts, athletics, and community service develop well-rounded individuals prepared to make a difference in the world.
